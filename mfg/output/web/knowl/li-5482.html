We isolate the logarithm, then rewrite the equation in exponential form:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] 2(\log_{3} (x)) \amp = 5\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by 5.}}\\ \log_{3} (x) \amp = \frac{5}{2}\amp\amp \blert{\text{Convert to exponential form.}}\\ x \amp = 3^{5/2} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
